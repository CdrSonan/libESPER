Detailed Description

NFFT-based discrete Radon transform.

Computes the discrete Radon transform

by taking the 2D-NFFT of ( ) at the points of the polar or linogram grid followed by 1D-iFFTs for every direction , where are the weights of the Dirichlet- or Fejer-kernel.
